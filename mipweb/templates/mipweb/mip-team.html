ACS-ERC Core Team

Dr. Lemba D. Nyirenda

He is the Lead Researcher and Inventor of the NP = P Transform. Lemba graduated from the University of Zambia in 1998 with a Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical Engineering with major in Electrical Machines and Power. He then went to the University of California Irvine, USA, where he obtained a Masters of Science in Power Systems and a PhD in Electrical Engineering with his research focussing on Operational Research. He has vast experience in industry, research and academia. He holds a patent in an algorithm which resolves NP = P Transform.


Mr. Julien M. Shabani

He is a researcher and IT Specilist in Software Engineering and Cyber Security. Julien graduated from the University of Zambia in 1998 with a Bachelor of Engineering in Electrical Engineering with major in Electronics and Telecommunications. In 2008, he obtained a Masters of Science in Information Security from Royal Holloway, University of London, UK. He has more than 20 years of experience in Information Technology (IT); covering software engineering, Cyber Security, and Enterprise Risk Management (ERM). He is currently working as a Lecturer in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at the University of Zambia. His research interests are in Cyber Security, Artificial Intelligence and Risk Management for resilience of Critical Infrastructures.
